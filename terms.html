Our position is unusual: Most privacy policies explain what data a company collects. Ours mostly explains what we don't collect, because the list of things we do collect is very short.

How your screenshots are processed

When you upload a screenshot, the following happens:

  • Your image is sent to our serverless function running on Netlify
  • The image is transmitted to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis
  • Claude generates a report based on the visible content of your screenshot
  • The report is returned to your browser
  • The image is immediately deleted from our systems ‚Äî it is not written to any persistent storage at any point
  • The report exists only in your browser session ‚Äî once you close the page, it's gone

Your screenshots are processed by Anthropic's Claude API, which is subject to Anthropic's privacy policy. Under Anthropic's API terms, data submitted via the API is not used to train their models.

Listing Lens is a marketplace listing analysis tool. You upload a screenshot of a listing from any online marketplace. We use artificial intelligence to analyse the visible content and generate a buyer intelligence report highlighting potential issues, hidden costs, and questions to consider.

How it works

  • Free scan: Upload one screenshot. Our AI identifies the listing and provides a summary of what it found ‚Äî the category, estimated issue count, and what the full report would cover. This is free and costs you nothing.
  • Paid report ($5 AUD): If the scan shows value, you can pay $5 to unlock a full report. After payment, you're taken to a temporary workspace where you upload up to two screenshots of the same listing. Our AI cross-references both images and generates a detailed buyer intelligence report.
